Our Vision

To equip God's people to establish an authentic relationship with Him and experience the fulness of His power and love. We strive to become a church that reflects the heart of Christ.

Our Mission

ACP enables individuals to experience a truth encounter and power encounter evidenced by clear declaration of God's Spirit and power. We bring glory to God by obeying His word and loving Him and others through:

Worship

Loving God with all our hearts, demonstrated through worshipping Him in Spirit and truth (John 4:23-24, 1 Peter 2:9)

Ministry

Loving our neighbours as ourselves, demonstrated by loving one another and ministering (service) to others (Galatians 5:14 & Luke 4:18)

Evangelism

Winning the unsaved for Christ, demonstrated by sharing God's plan of salvation through evangelism of our relatives, friends and individuals within our communities and workplaces (Mark 16:15, Luke 24:47, Acts 2:38)

Fellowship

Fellowshipping with Christian believers, demonstrated through prayer, regular church service attendance, and other sanctioned events (Heb 10:25, Eph 2:19, Acts 2:42)

Discipleship

Making disciples, demonstrated by teaching believers to be more like Jesus Christ through obedience to God's word and leading them to spiritual maturity (Colossians 1:28, Eph 4:12-13)
